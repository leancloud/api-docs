queryWithClassName:predicate:

Creates a AVQuery with the constraints given by predicate.

+ (instancetype)queryWithClassName:(NSString *)className predicate:(NSPredicate *)predicate

Parameters

className

the class name

predicate

the predicates

Discussion

Creates a AVQuery with the constraints given by predicate.

The following types of predicates are supported: * Simple comparisons such as =, !=, <, >, <=, >=, and BETWEEN with a key and a constant. * Containment predicates, such as “x IN {1, 2, 3}”. * Key-existence predicates, such as “x IN SELF”. * BEGINSWITH expressions. * Compound predicates with AND, OR, and NOT. * SubQueries with “key IN %@”, subquery.

The following types of predicates are NOT supported: * Aggregate operations, such as ANY, SOME, ALL, or NONE. * Regular expressions, such as LIKE, MATCHES, CONTAINS, or ENDSWITH. * Predicates comparing one key to another. * Complex predicates with many ORed clauses.
